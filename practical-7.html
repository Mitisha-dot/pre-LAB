Dr. A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India. He was a great scientist and known as the "Missile Man of India" because of his contribution to ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.He was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram,Tamil Nadu,India.

He inspired millions of students with his knowledge, simplicity and dedication to the nation.
